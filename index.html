How to perfectly cook a Steak

Contents:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Temperature Chart

The only recipe you'll need!

  • Time: 30m
  • Serves: 1-4

Ingredients Quantity
Grab a GOOD steak cut One Steak
Butter A quarter of a stick
Salt and Pepper Enough to Season
Olive Oil Enough To Oil Pan
Any Other Flavorings you may need Like Basil,Seasonings,Garlic,Tyme

Step 1: Take out your steak, let it rest until its about at room temperature.
Step 2: Pat dry your steak, and season generously, to give the steak a delicous crust.
Step 3: Grab a cast iron skillet, and get it SMOKING hot. This helps cook the outer layers to perfection.
Step 4: Use olive oil and then add the steak. Cook each side for about 30 seconds each including the sides.
Step 5: Add your tyme, butter, and any other flavorings you want to baste your steak with.
Step 6: Cook until slightly below your desired temperature.(SEE CHART BELOW.) Let your steak rest for 5-10mins to let all the juice soak up.
